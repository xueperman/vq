Our Programs

Guaranteed Salary Program

Our Guaranteed Salary Program is perfect for veterinarians who want to commit to a certain number of shifts per week or month. Your salary is GUARANTEED, so even if a clinic cancels your shift, you still get paid. Choose between either full-time or part-time.

Full-Time

This program requires vets to commit to 3 shifts per week or 12 shifts per month. You can always pick up more!

Part-Time

This program requires vets to commit to 8 shifts per month. You can always pick up more!
