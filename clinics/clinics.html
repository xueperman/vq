How to get your shifts filled

We bridge the gap between Relief Veterinarians and clinics. When you become one of our Clinic Partners, we make sure that all your relief shifts are covered.
Add in steps BELOW:

Step 1

One of our Clinic Partners reaches out about a shift they need covered

Step 2

Our Staffing Team speaks to our Veterinarians that we have in that area and makes sure their skills set and personality is the right fit for your clinic

Step 3

Our Staffing Team sends you a confirmation email with who will be covering that shift and sends you their pertinent documents

Step 4

One of our Veterinarian’s shows up to cover that shift

Benefits of Partnering With Us

  • We take care of payroll and invoicing
  • We provide our full-time veterinarians the option for full benefits (medical/ dental/ and vision, etc.).
  • We build our veterinarian’s schedules, which means if you find a veterinarian that is a great fit for your clinic, we can provide continuous coverage for your needs.
  • We are nationwide so if we have to cancel coverage, we always try our best to find a replacement
  • You can reach us 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for all questions and concerns
