Different types of travel opportunities

Our Guaranteed Salary Travel Program is perfect for the veterinarian who wants to do a short travel assignment, wants to travel somewhere new, or wants to venture outside of their city. To be considered for our Travel Program, you must be willing to travel 50 or more miles for shifts. Your salary is Guaranteed, so even if a clinic cancels your shift, you still get paid.

  • Our Full-Time Guaranteed Salary Program requires veterinarians to commit to 3 shifts per week or 12 shifts per month. You can always pick up more!
  • Our Part-Time Guaranteed Salary Program requires veterinarians to commit to 8 shifts per month. You can always pick up more!
  • If you cannot commit to 8 shifts per month, then the IC Program is perfect for you. Pick up shifts when you have the free time and still be able to use your multiple licenses.

Benefits of Our Travel Program

  • Travel assignments can last anywhere from 4 – 13 weeks, so if you are looking for a change of scenery, this program is perfect for you
  • You receive an untaxed travel stipend
  • Full-time veterinarians receive: Medical/Dental/ Vision coverage, 401(k) Savings Program, PTO Program, and continuing education credits
  • Full-time and Part-time veterinarians receive ownership in the company through stock options and a retention bonus
